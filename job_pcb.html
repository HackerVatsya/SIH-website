Careers in Science (PCB)

Opportunities in healthcare, life sciences, and research.

Doctor / Physician / Surgeon

Diagnoses, treats and manages patient care; specialization possible.

Requires: MBBS Degree (via NEET)

Dentist / Orthodontist

Treats dental issues, oral surgery and orthodontics.

Requires: BDS Degree

Ayurvedic / Homeopathic / Unani Practitioner

Provides traditional medicine and therapy in respective systems.

Requires: BAMS, BHMS, or BUMS Degree

Staff Nurse / Clinical Nurse

Provides nursing care and supports patient recovery.

Requires: B.Sc Nursing or GNM Diploma

Physiotherapist

Rehabilitates patients to restore mobility and function.

Requires: BPT Degree

Occupational Therapist

Helps patients regain daily living skills.

Requires: BOT Degree

Pharmacist / Clinical Pharmacist

Dispenses medicines and advises on pharmacotherapy.

Requires: B.Pharm Degree

Medical Laboratory Technician (MLT)

Performs diagnostic tests and lab analyses.

Requires: B.Sc MLT or Diploma

Radiographer / Imaging Technologist

Operates imaging equipment for diagnostics.

Requires: B.Sc Radiography or Diploma

Ultrasound Technician / Sonographer

Performs ultrasound imaging for diagnostics.

Requires: Diploma or Certificate

Veterinarian

Provides medical care and surgery for animals.

Requires: B.V.Sc Degree

Clinical Researcher

Conducts clinical trials, medical research and data analysis.

Requires: Life Science Degree / B.Pharm

Dietitian / Nutritionist

Provides dietary advice for health and disease management.

Requires: B.Sc Nutrition / Dietetics

Biotechnologist

Uses biological systems for research and product development.

Requires: B.Sc / B.Tech Biotech

Forensic Scientist

Analyzes crime scene evidence for legal investigations.

Requires: B.Sc Forensic Science

Agricultural Scientist / Agronomist

Researches crop production and soil management.

Requires: B.Sc Agriculture

Horticulturist

Cultivates plants, fruits, and manages gardens/nurseries.

Requires: B.Sc Horticulture / Agriculture

Operation Theatre Technician

Assists surgical teams in operating rooms.

Requires: Diploma / B.Sc OTT

Dental Hygienist / Assistant

Assists dentists and provides preventative dental care.

Requires: Diploma / Certificate

Environmental Health Technician

Monitors environmental pollutants and public health impacts.

Requires: B.Sc Env. Science / Public Health

Toxicology Lab Technician

Performs toxicological analyses in labs and forensics.

Requires: B.Sc / Diploma

Clinical Technician / Lab Analyst

Performs clinical biochemistry tests and data recording.

Requires: B.Sc Clinical BioChem / MLT

Plant Breeder Assistant / Agritech Field Officer

Supports crop breeding and field-level agritech implementations.

Requires: B.Sc Agriculture or Diploma

Diagnostics / Lab Startup Founder

Starts diagnostic services, labs or health-tech solutions.

Requires: Degree + Short Courses

Psychiatric Technician / Assistant

Supports psychiatric clinicians in treatment settings.

Requires: B.Sc Psychology / Diploma

Animal Caretaker / Kennel Assistant

Assists in animal care facilities and shelters.

Requires: 12th Pass / Apprenticeship

Health Educator / Community Health Worker

Provides basic health education and community outreach.

Requires: 12th Pass + Short Course

Molecular Diagnostics Technician

Performs PCR and molecular testing for diseases.

Requires: B.Sc Molecular Diagnostics / Diploma
