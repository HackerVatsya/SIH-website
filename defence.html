Top Defence Institutions

Institutions dedicated to training the future leadership of the armed forces.

National Defence Academy (NDA)

Khadakwasla, Maharashtra

The premier joint defence service training institute of the Indian Armed Forces, where cadets of the three services train together before they go on to respective service academies for further pre-commissioning training.

Indian Military Academy (IMA)

Dehradun, Uttarakhand

The IMA trains officers for the Indian Army. Cadets undergo rigorous training to develop leadership qualities, mental and physical toughness, and strategic knowledge.

Air Force Academy (AFA)

Dundigal, Telangana

The AFA is the cradle of training for pilots, ground duty, and technical officers of the Indian Air Force. Cadets are trained in flying, navigation, and other specialized streams.

Officers Training Academy (OTA)

Chennai, Tamil Nadu

The OTA trains men and women for the Short Service Commission in the Indian Army. It is the only institution that commissions female officers into the combat support arms of the Army.

Pune, Maharashtra

AFMC is a premier medical college in India, acknowledged as a centre of excellence for education and research. It provides medical training to undergraduate and post-graduate medical and nursing students with assured career prospects in the defence services.
