Careers in Science (PCM)

Opportunities in technology, engineering, and physical sciences.

Electrician (Residential / Industrial)

Installs, maintains, and repairs electrical systems and wiring.

Requires: 12th / ITI / Diploma

Plumber / Pipefitter

Installs and repairs plumbing systems, piping and fixtures.

Requires: 12th / ITI / Diploma

Welder / Fabricator

Performs welding and fabricating metal parts for structures and machines.

Requires: 12th / Short Course (Welding)

Fitter / Machinist

Assembles, fits and maintains mechanical parts/machines.

Requires: 12th / ITI / Diploma

Mechanical Technician / Junior Engineer

Supports mechanical design, maintenance and manufacturing processes.

Requires: Polytechnic / Diploma / B.Tech

Mechanical Design Engineer

Designs mechanical systems, components and products.

Requires: B.Tech (Mechanical)

Automotive / EV Engineer

Designs and works on conventional and electric vehicle systems.

Requires: B.Tech (Mechanical) / EV Diploma

Automotive Technician / Mechanic

Maintains and repairs vehicles; specialization in engines, transmissions.

Requires: ITI / Diploma

Software Developer / Engineer

Writes, tests and maintains code for software applications and systems.

Requires: BCA / B.Tech (CS/IT)

Web Developer (Frontend/Backend)

Builds and maintains websites and web applications.

Requires: BCA / B.Tech (CS/IT)

Data Analyst / Scientist

Analyzes data to extract insights and build predictive models.

Requires: B.Sc (CS/IT) / BCA / B.Tech

Architect

Designs buildings and urban spaces, prepares blueprints.

Requires: B.Arch

Civil Engineer

Designs and manages infrastructure projects like roads, bridges.

Requires: Diploma (Civil) / B.Tech (Civil)

Electrical / Electronics Engineer

Designs and develops electrical systems and electronic circuits.

Requires: B.Tech (EEE/ECE) / Diploma

Indian Army / Navy / Air Force Officer

Leads and manages operations in the armed forces.

Requires: NDA / Defence pathway

Aircraft Maintenance Engineer (AME)

Maintains and certifies aircraft for flight safety.

Requires: AME License / DGCA

Physicist / Research Scientist

Conducts research in physics and scientific domains.

Requires: B.Sc (Physics/Maths) / B.Tech

Chemist / Lab Analyst

Performs chemical analysis and research in labs.

Requires: B.Sc (Chemistry) / B.Tech (Chem)

Aerospace / Aeronautical Engineer

Designs aircraft, spacecraft, and related systems.

Requires: B.Tech (Aeronautical)

Tech Startup Founder / Entrepreneur

Starts technology-based businesses or product ventures.

Requires: B.Tech / Entrepreneurship

Textile Technician / Process Engineer

Works in textile manufacturing and process optimization.

Requires: Diploma / B.Tech (Textile)

Industrial / Product Designer

Designs physical products, prototypes and manufacturing details.

Requires: B.Des / B.Sc Design

Elevator / Escalator Technician

Maintains and repairs vertical transportation systems.

Requires: Short Course / Electrician Cert

Refrigeration & AC Technician

Installs and services refrigeration and AC systems.

Requires: ITI / Diploma

Warehouse / Forklift Operator

Operates forklifts and handles warehouse logistics.

Requires: 12th / Apprenticeship

CAD / CAM Operator

Creates CAD models and CAM toolpaths for manufacturing.

Requires: Short Courses / Certifications

Data Entry Operator / Back-office

Performs data entry and clerical data processing tasks.

Requires: 12th / Short Course

Control & Instrumentation Engineer

Works on control systems, PLCs and instrumentation.

Requires: B.Tech (Electronics) / Diploma

Bioprocess Technician / Biomanufacturing

Supports biotech production and fermentation processes.

Requires: B.Sc (Biotech) / B.Tech (Biotech)
