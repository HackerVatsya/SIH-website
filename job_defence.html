Govt, Defence & Vocational Careers

Opportunities open to students from all academic backgrounds.

Civil Services (IAS/IPS/IFS)

Administrative leadership and policy implementation in government.

Requires: UPSC (Any Degree)

Indian Army / Air Force Officer (NDA)

Commissioned officer roles in defence services.

Requires: NDA / 12th

Agniveer / Soldier

Short-term service roles in the armed forces.

Requires: Agniveer / 12th

Inspector / Auditor (SSC CGL)

Government administrative and specialist roles in ministries.

Requires: SSC CGL / Graduation

Clerk / Data Entry Operator (SSC CHSL)

Clerical and back-office roles in government offices.

Requires: SSC CHSL / 12th

Bank PO / Clerk

Banking roles in public sector banks and financial institutions.

Requires: IBPS / SBI (Graduation)

Railway Technician / Goods Guard

Operational and technical roles in Indian Railways.

Requires: RRB (Railways) / 10th-12th/ITI

Assistant Commandant (CAPF)

Leadership roles in paramilitary forces like BSF, CRPF.

Requires: CAPF Exams / Degree

Coast Guard Officer / Sailor

Maritime security and coastal operations roles.

Requires: Indian Coast Guard / 12th-Grad

Gramin Dak Sevak / Postman

Postal delivery and rural postal services.

Requires: Postal Circle / 10th-12th

Electrician (Residential/Industrial)

Installs, maintains and repairs electrical systems.

Requires: ITI Electrician

Fitter / Machinist

Assembles and maintains mechanical components and machines.

Requires: ITI Fitter

Automotive Technician / Mechanic

Maintains and repairs vehicles; EV specialization possible.

Requires: Automotive Diploma / ITI

Solar PV Installer / Technician

Installs and maintains solar PV systems.

Requires: Solar PV Installer Course

EV Technician

Maintains electric vehicle powertrains and batteries.

Requires: Electric Vehicle Tech Course

Web Developer / Coder

Builds and maintains websites and web applications.

Requires: Web Dev Bootcamp

PLC / Automation Technician

Maintains PLCs and industrial automation systems.

Requires: Automation / PLC Course

Mobile Phone Repair Technician

Repairs and services smartphones and electronics.

Requires: Mobile Repair Course
